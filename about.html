My goal is to make delicious vegetarian food affordable and available for everyone. I first found my passion for cooking while traveling in Italy in college. I knew then and there that I wanted to connect my passion for environmentalism with my newfound passion for cooking by creating a business as a vegetarian personal chef. Contact me to have a healthy, delicious meal cooked in your home. Whether it is for a relaxing night in or to entertain guests, leave the cooking to me!
